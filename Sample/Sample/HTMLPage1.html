Description

Reliance Capital Ltd. (Reliance Capital) is a financial services company. The Company has interests in asset management and mutual funds; life and general insurance; commercial and home finance; stock broking; wealth management services; distribution of financial products; private equity; asset reconstruction; investments and other activities in financial services. The Company operates in five segments: Finance and Investment, which includes the corporate lending and investment activities; Asset Management, which includes the asset management activities including Mutual Fund and Portfolio Management Services; General Insurance, which includes the general insurance business; Commercial Finance, which includes the commercial finance and home finance business and Others, which includes other financial and allied services.
